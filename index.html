About me

After graduating in architecture, I discovered my passion for design and decided to focus my career on graphic design. However, my curiosity and eagerness to learn led me to explore new opportunities in the field of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design. Attracted by the combination of aesthetics and functionality, I decided to turn my career around and dive into the world of digital design. I trained in the latest design trends and gained knowledge in specialized tools and software to carry out app and website design projects.

Recently, I have further expanded my skills by completing a course in frontend development. I can now not only design great experiences with attractive interfaces, but also bring my designs to life. My frontend experience allows me to understand technical limitations and collaborate effectively with developers to achieve an optimal user experience.
